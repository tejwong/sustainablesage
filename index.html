About

Design rooted in natural materials, calm composition, and everyday beauty.

Shawn Wong Designs approaches each project with a belief that luxurious spaces do not need to feel formal or overdesigned. Instead, the work emphasizes light, texture, craftsmanship, and a lasting connection between home, landscape, and the way people actually live.

Inspired by relaxed California living and elevated by a sustainability-minded lens, the studio creates interiors and outdoor rooms that are layered yet serene. Projects begin with listening: how the home should feel, how the family moves through it, and how design can support both beauty and ease.

Materials are chosen for softness, durability, and authenticity—limewash tones, warm woods, textural stone, handmade finishes, and pieces that age gracefully over time. Outdoor spaces are treated as extensions of the home, with an emphasis on natural planting, gathering, and flow.

The result is a tailored environment that feels timeless rather than trend-driven: comfortable, curated, and deeply connected to place.

— Shawn Wong
Services

Indoor and outdoor design services crafted for homes that feel cohesive and intentional.

From room-level refinements to larger property transformations, Shawn Wong Designs offers a holistic design approach that brings architecture, furnishings, and landscape into quiet balance.

Interior Design

Thoughtful interiors that balance comfort, elegance, and function with layered materials and a restrained, elevated palette.

  • Space planning and furniture layouts
  • Material, paint, and finish selections
  • Lighting, styling, and sourcing guidance
  • Kitchen, bath, and full-home aesthetic direction

Outdoor Design

Outdoor spaces designed as natural extensions of the home, with attention to beauty, climate, durability, and daily enjoyment.

  • Landscape concept direction
  • Patio, terrace, and outdoor room planning
  • Drought-conscious and sustainable planting ideas
  • Hardscape, furnishings, and styling recommendations

Design Consultation

Flexible guidance for clients who want expert direction, a strong visual point of view, and a clear next step for their project.

  • Virtual or in-person design sessions

  • Material palettes and mood boards
  • Project prioritization and design roadmaps
